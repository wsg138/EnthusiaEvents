# Testing Checklist

## Races

- [ ] Confirm Boat Race boats do not collide.
- [ ] Confirm Elytra checkpoint setup works end to end.
- [ ] Confirm Elytra checkpoints register only in order.
- [ ] Confirm Elytra players cannot finish without completing every checkpoint.
- [ ] Confirm Elytra checkpoint respawns work correctly.

## Red Light Green Light

- [ ] Confirm setup can save the light display and a separate long finish-line area.
- [ ] Confirm moving on red eliminates the player.
- [ ] Confirm crossing the finish line on green records the player as finished.
- [ ] Confirm first place remains the winner while other players can continue finishing.
- [ ] Confirm the event ends when everyone finishes or when the timer expires.

## Combat

- [ ] Confirm Quake shots cannot pass through walls.
- [ ] Confirm Quake death sound and respawn countdown work.
- [ ] Confirm CTF deaths restore the kit in the same slots.
- [ ] Confirm CTF flags can be captured, dropped, and returned.
- [ ] Confirm Fight and Sumo brackets run two players at a time.
- [ ] Confirm Fight kit voting items appear for 1v1, 2v2, and FFA.
- [ ] Confirm Fight kit voting, previews, and selected kits work.

## BedWars

- [ ] Confirm the Solo-style item shop layout, prices, and purchase sounds.
- [ ] Confirm permanent armor and team upgrades persist after respawning.
- [ ] Confirm Sharpness, Protection, Haste, Forge, Heal Pool, and traps work.
- [ ] Confirm players are eliminated after their bed is broken.
- [ ] Confirm placed TNT automatically ignites.
- [ ] Confirm TNT and fireballs only destroy player-placed blocks.
- [ ] Confirm fireballs launch, explode, and apply knockback.
- [ ] Confirm the BedWars scoreboard columns are aligned and readable.
- [ ] Confirm BedWars lasts 20 minutes.
- [ ] Confirm every remaining bed is destroyed after 15 minutes.
- [ ] Confirm dropped BedWars items are cleared when the event resets.
- [ ] Confirm killers receive the victim's iron, gold, diamonds, and emeralds.
